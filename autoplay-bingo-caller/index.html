    How the Autoplay Bingo Caller Works

    This autoplay bingo caller is designed to automatically call bingo numbers at a steady pace without manual input. By using timed intervals, hosts can run a full bingo game hands free while players focus on listening and watching the board.

    • Click the large call area to start the bingo game.
    • Enable Autoplay to activate automatic number calling.
    • Select a timed interval such as 3s, 5s, 8s, 10s, or 15s.
    • Turn on Announcer Voice for spoken number calls.
    • Use Fullscreen mode for TVs, projectors, and large displays.

    The automatic bingo caller tracks all called numbers and highlights them on the 75 ball grid, making it easy for players to follow along.

    Features of the Automatic Bingo Caller

    This automatic bingo caller includes adjustable autoplay speeds, optional voice announcements, and a clear 75 ball bingo grid. As a hands free bingo caller, it reduces the need for constant interaction and keeps games running smoothly.

    The timed bingo caller works entirely in your browser with no downloads or setup required. Autoplay and voice settings can be turned on or off at any time and are remembered for future games.
