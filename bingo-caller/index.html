    What Is a Bingo Caller?

    A bingo caller is a tool used to randomly select and announce bingo numbers during a game. This online bingo caller replaces traditional physical cages and cards by providing a clear digital call display that players can easily follow.

    This bingo caller is designed for simplicity, visibility, and reliability. It works directly in your browser and can be used for classrooms, senior centers, community events, fundraisers, and family bingo games.

    How to Use the Bingo Caller

    Using the bingo caller is simple and requires no setup. The large call display and straightforward controls make it easy to run a game without distractions.

    • Click the large call area to begin calling numbers.
    • Use Next to advance calls manually.
    • Enable Autoplay to create an automatic bingo caller.
    • Turn on Announcer Voice to hear numbers spoken aloud.
    • Use Fullscreen mode for TVs, projectors, and shared displays.

    All called numbers are tracked automatically and highlighted on the 75 ball grid so players can follow along throughout the game.
